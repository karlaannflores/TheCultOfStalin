What was the purpose of the cult of Stalin?

  • The Cult of Personality worshipped Stalin.

  • They looked up to him, primarily because of the propaganda in newspapers.

  • Considering that Stalin controlled how they thought through the newspapers, it was hard to see what the rest of the world saw.

What were the methods used to portray Stalin in a god-like light?

  • Buildings were covered with giant portraits of his face.
  • Shop windows displayed pictures of him.
  • Towns, cities, rivers, canals, schools, hospitals, mountains and lakes were named after Stalin.
  • Films, plays, poems, stories and novels were created to celebrate his life.
  • Stalin utilized the newspapers as well.
